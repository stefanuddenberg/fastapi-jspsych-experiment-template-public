Purpose of the Study:

This study examines people's mental images of what [[&condition_description]] looks like. On each screen, you were shown a face and asked to select whether the person looked like [[&condition_description]]. These faces were generated by a very particular random process so that, if we average together all the images you and other people choose, we will be able to get a better idea of what all sorts of people believe [[&condition_description]] looks like â€” perhaps people with different political beliefs, demographics, etc. have very different images in their heads! In addition, other participants may have been asked to consider what different types of people look like, or may have seen different faces to choose from.

As this study is ongoing, we ask that you not discuss this study with anyone else who is currently participating or might participate at a future time.
